Our Services

Aerial Survey

High-accuracy aerial mapping for large-scale terrains using photogrammetry and advanced data processing.

How It Works

Mission Planning & Data Acquisition

We deploy drones equipped with high-resolution sensors to capture precise aerial or underwater data based on project requirements.

Processing & Geospatial Modeling

Collected data is processed using photogrammetry, LiDAR, or specialized geospatial tools to generate accurate 2D/3D outputs.

Analysis & Quality Verification

Our experts validate every dataset and perform advanced analytics to extract actionable insights and ensure high accuracy.

Reporting & Delivery

Clients receive interactive maps, dashboards, or CAD/BIM-ready files with full project documentation and recommendations.

Benefits

Higher Accuracy & Reliability

Precision-driven data ensures confident decision-making across all engineering, construction, and inspection workflows.

Faster Project Timelines

Automated drone capture and rapid processing reduce project timelines significantly compared to traditional methods.

Reduced Operational Risk

Minimize human exposure to hazardous environments through remote aerial/underwater data collection.

Cost-Optimized Operations

Eliminate manual surveying delays and reworks with real-time, high-quality geospatial insights.

Seamless Integration with Existing Systems

Deliverables align with CAD, GIS, BIM, and enterprise data platforms for easy integration.

Who We Are

Shamal Technologies is a leading provider of advanced drone solutions, delivering real-time geospatial intelligence for diverse industries. Our team of experts leverages cutting-edge drone technology to offer actionable insights and enhance decision-making processes.

With years of experience in environmental monitoring, construction, mining, and asset inspection, we ensure precise and reliable results every time.

Our mission is to combine technology and expertise to accelerate projects, reduce operational risks, and optimize cost efficiency for our clients worldwide.

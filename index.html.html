ABOUT US

Pioneering Geospatial Intelligence

Shamal Technologies is a leading provider of advanced drone solutions, delivering real-time geospatial intelligence for diverse industries.

Our core mission is to empower decision-makers with high-accuracy, reliable data captured via cutting-edge drone technology and processed using state-of-the-art geospatial analyses. We specialize in reducing project timelines, mitigating risks, and optimizing operational costs.

With years of experience across construction, mining, and environmental sectors, our team ensures every deliverable is precise, actionable, and ready for integration into your existing enterprise systems (CAD, GIS, BIM).

OUR PROCESS

How We Deliver Results

1. Planning & Acquisition

Deploy drones equipped with high-resolution sensors to capture precise aerial or underwater data based on project requirements.

2. Modeling & Processing

Data is processed using photogrammetry, LiDAR, or specialized geospatial tools to generate accurate 2D/3D models and outputs.

3. Analysis & Verification

Experts validate every dataset and perform advanced analytics to extract actionable insights and ensure sub-centimeter accuracy.

4. Delivery & Reporting

Clients receive interactive maps, dashboards, or CAD/BIM-ready files with full documentation and recommendations.
